Why I built QuickDoc

In 2023, I moved from my village to Delhi. I had all my documents — but my date of birth on school records didn't match my Aadhaar. 11 schools refused my admission.

I spent months visiting schools, meeting education officials, and even approaching the education department. Nothing worked. I lost one full year of education. One year of my life — not because I didn't deserve it, but because India's system has no trusted, transparent support for ordinary people.

“No student should lose education. No citizen should lose opportunities. No family should suffer because of document problems.”

That is why I built QuickDoc — a platform where you can update your Aadhaar, correct your PAN, or apply for a Passport without agents, without WhatsApp, without stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

🔒 Is my Aadhaar number safe with QuickDoc? ▼
Yes. We never store your full Aadhaar number — we only keep a masked version (XXXX XXXX 1234). All documents are deleted after 15 days of service completion.
⏱ How long does Aadhaar update take? ▼
Typically 3–7 days from the date of submission. This depends on UIDAI processing times.
💰 What if my documents are rejected? ▼
We will notify you immediately and help you correct the issue. If we cannot proceed, you will get a full refund.
📄 What documents do I need for Aadhaar update? ▼
You need: 1) Your current Aadhaar card (front & back photo), 2) Address proof (Voter ID, Passport, or Utility bill), 3) Registered mobile number for OTP.
💳 How do I pay? ▼
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Card, and Netbanking via Razorpay — a 2% transaction fee applies.
🔄 Can I cancel my request? ▼
100% refund if work hasn't started within 24 hours. 50% refund if work started but not submitted. No refund after UIDAI submission.
